About Us

Where Community
Meets Competition

Access Padel is New Jersey's premier destination for padel — the racquet sport that blends tennis, squash, and pure social energy into something you have to experience to understand.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Hillsborough features professional-grade courts, co-working spaces, and a community of players from every background and skill level. Whether you've never held a racket or you're training for your next tournament, this is your home court.
